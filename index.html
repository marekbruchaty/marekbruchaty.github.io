About

When it comes to design, truly great design needs to combine beauty & simplicity with functionality. pure black. is an icon set that fully utilizes your phones OLED display, maximizing the amount of black on your screen and thus preserving as much battery power as possible. It’s efficient, yet simple & beautiful. Through iOS Shortcuts, you can utilize these custom icons however you want, making your iOS experience more enjoyable.
